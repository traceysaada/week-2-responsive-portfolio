The 1960's

The Decade I was Born

This was the Decade of the Beatles, Rock & roll, Elvis Presley, people just wanted to dance & go to the Flicks. A time for Hippies & free love, Twiggy & skinny models, hour glass figures on the way out, people became more into following various Fashions of the time including Drugs, Sex & Rock & Roll. Class structure started to breakdown & women gained more control of their own lives.

The 1970's

My Teenage Years

Secondary School, Disco's on a Friday night, mostley in our schools of youth clubs & we all knew the dance routines. Clothes length was maxi, mini & midi, so new Fashion's bellbottom Trousers & plateform shoes, We all got to be taller.

It was in the 1970's I left school, started by first job & learnt to become a chef. I got married & we got our First Female Prime Minister The Iron Lady, Margaret Thatcher.

The 1980's

The Year that changed my Life Forever

Motherhood, the birth of my 3 Daughters, my greatest achievement, my greatest joy & my biggest weakness.

something we hope we would never see again happend THE FAULKLANDS WAR, fleets of our Navy war ships sailing out from our shores to the other side of the world, carrying our airforce, our soldiers. We are showing the world our force & finding ourselves with an enemy we didnt know we had for an island that was more their neighbour than ours.
